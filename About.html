About us

History

Established in 2013, TCS first specialized in quality assurance consultancy. It quickly evolved and added a 4-scope-calibration wing to the company. Test Calibration Services ltd employees are trained to keep pace with industry trends, emerging technology and client needs.

   
                 

Our Work

In the present day industrial environment, it is very important to ascertain the weight of product to avoid wastage and compromised product quality. The instruments used to achieve confidence in weight readings have to be routinely checked for conformation to National Standards. Such checking will be profitable to an organization in avoiding economic loss and ensuring consumer protection. This will be good in keeping good manufacturing practices.
